COVID-19 Vaccines

Notes

Vaccine data is published weekly by NHS England by NHS ICS/STP. We've used ONS population estimates for CCGs (mid-2019) to calculate how many people are under 80 and how many are over 80 in each ICS/STP. This allows us to work out the percentage of each age group vaccinated.
