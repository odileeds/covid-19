Notes

NHS England publish vaccine data weekly by NHS Improvement integrated care systems (ICS) and sustainability and transformation partnerships (STP). They publish count of vaccinations by age band (not all age bands - it varies week-to-week) by dose and by Integrated Care Systems (ICSs)/Sustainability and Transformation Partnerships (STPs). We've used ONS population estimates for CCGs (mid-2019) We've used an LSOA to STP lookup (April 2021), an LSOA to MSOA lookup (2011), and the NHS England NIMS population by MSOA to calculate how many people are in the different age categories in each ICS/STP. This allows us to work out the percentage of each age group vaccinated. As we are now using NIMS 2021 population values instead of 2019 ONS estimates, this should avoid some categories showing greater than 100% (sometimes up to 125%). In the maps, yellow means more and purple means less.

From 27th January all data given by NHS England will include vaccinations administered in Hospital Hubs, Local Vaccination Service sites (e.g. GP practices) and Vaccination Centres. This data is as reported from the National Immunisation Management Service (NIMS), and is classified as management information. NIMS is the System of Record for the NHS COVID-19 vaccination programme in England.
