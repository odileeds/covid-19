Graphs of weekly deaths in England & Wales for 2020 and 2021. Data up to week ending 23rd April 2021.

See graphs for: All ages | under 65 | 65-79 | under 80 | 80+

Notes

These figures represent death registrations, there can be a delay between the date a death occurred and the date a death was registered.

Moveable public holidays, when register offices are closed, affect the number of registrations made in the published weeks and in the corresponding weeks in previous years. The average for 2015-2019 provides a comparison of the number of deaths expected per week in a usual (non-pandemic) year.

You should also note that ONS weeks don't match with ISO8601 definition of weeks and don't start on the same day of the year each year. So exact week comparison between years may not be appropriate.
