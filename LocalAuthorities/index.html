Deprecated: 2020-07-31

This visualisation operated from March to July 2020. It relied on the excellent UK-wide local-level data compiled by Tom White. Tom is, understandably, no longer updating that dataset. He has done an excellent volunteer job over the past five months maintaining this data in a consistent manner regardless of how England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland have moved or changed what they do. This visualisation wouldn't have been practical without Tom's efforts. Ideally someone in central Government would be compiling consistent, local-level, time-series data across the whole of the UK. Meanwhile, we still have a variety of local authority level hexmaps showing other COVID-19-related data.

About this dashboard

This dashboard is created by ODI Leeds to help Local Authorities (and similar) get an overview of how their area compares to other parts of the United Kingdom. The code is released under an MIT Licence on Github. Please add issues on Github.
